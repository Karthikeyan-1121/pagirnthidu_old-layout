About Pagirnthidu

India's first charitable trust with a vision to uplift aspiring individuals only who have the capability and the willingness to help others. The foundation serves as a platform for sharing materials, donations, and volunteering for the right needs with the givers and receivers who are about to make a positive impact.

Why Pagirnthidu?

Pagirnthidu, which translates to "Rise Together" in Tamil, encapsulates its core philosophy: the elevation of aspiring individuals who are not only determined to better their own lives but are equally committed to extending a helping hand to others.

The vision is to create a community where empowerment, generosity, and a shared sense of responsibility coalesce to bring about positive change.

Answers to All Your Questions

Have any questions or need assistance? We’re here to help!

Why should I trust Pagirnthidu?
Pagirnthidu is a Government of India registered trust dedicated to empowering people and making a positive impact. We are committed to transparency and work diligently towards the betterment of those in need.
Why is Pagirnthidu better than other platforms?
Pagirnthidu stands out as a transparent platform where donors can trace the source and destination of their contributions. Donations made through Pagirnthidu consistently meet the intended needs of the beneficiaries.
Can I, as an individual, start a campaign?
Yes, anyone with a genuine need or the desire to support someone in need can initiate a campaign. However, approval is subject to verification.
How long will my campaign be posted on Pagirnthidu?
Campaigns will remain active until the required donations are fulfilled.
How will the raised funds be disbursed?
Funds will be disbursed in the form of cash or requested products, as specified by the campaign creator.
How do I donate to Pagirnthidu?
You can donate to Pagirnthidu by selecting a specific campaign or opting for a monthly donation plan.
Do I need to enter my PAN while donating?
Yes, PAN card details are required for donations through Pagirnthidu, as they may be used for tax deductions.
Is my donation on Pagirnthidu secure?
Absolutely. Donations on Pagirnthidu are securely processed through transparent payment gateways and structures.
What payment methods are accepted on Pagirnthidu?
Pagirnthidu accepts multiple payment methods, including Credit card, Debit Card, UPI, and Bank Transfers.
Will I receive a receipt for my monthly donation?
Yes, you will receive a receipt for your monthly donations in your registered email.
Can I get a refund for my donation?
No, donations made on Pagirnthidu are non-refundable as they are allocated for various social causes.
Do you have Pagirnthidu's monthly donation plans?
Yes, donors can unsubscribe from Pagirnthidu's Monthly Donation Plans at any time.
